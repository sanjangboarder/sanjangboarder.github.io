Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

I have been wearing Le Mouton shoes since last year. The brand markets their shoes based on wool fibers and comfort. Given their popularity, I wore them through the summer and winter seasons. As spring transitions to early summer, I wanted to find a lighter model for warm weather.

​

I searched the official website for a summer-weight model and ordered the shoes detailed below.

​

> **Honest Review Purchase Verification**
>
> *   **Product:** Le Mouton Forest Merino Wool Comfort Sneakers
> *   **Color:** Indigo Navy
> *   **Size:** 280mm
> *   **Purchase Status:** Confirmed (April 2026)

​

Having used Le Mouton shoes before, I selected size 280mm. I previously wore the Le Mouton Mate, but found it warm for summer use. Although the Mate is advertised as a four-season shoe, it was less suited for summer heat.

​

Therefore, I ordered the Forest model, which utilizes Merino wool for summer wear. Shipping was prompt, and the packaging was robust.

​

It arrived double-boxed, with the inner box being sturdy.

​

The shoes were secured inside the box, wrapped in protective paper.

​

The package includes a card detailing "H1-TEX" wool fabric technology. Le Mouton shoes utilize wool fibers. The material is lightweight and breathable, which improves fit comfort. My experience aligns with these claims.

​

The insole uses Ortholite technology, which is formulated to manage ventilation and retain shape. I did not experience odor issues while wearing the shoes, which suggests the insole is effective.

​

​

When discussing shoe comfort, manufacturers often highlight outsole technology, but Le Mouton does not focus heavily on this area. Based on my year of wearing Le Mouton shoes, the outsole durability is average, and the grip on wet surfaces is limited. While they perform well on dry pavements, caution is needed on wet or slippery surfaces.

​

I ordered the navy color model. While the outer upper looks like standard fabric, the interior contains a wool weave. I selected a model with a mesh-like outer pattern to maximize breathability for summer wear.
