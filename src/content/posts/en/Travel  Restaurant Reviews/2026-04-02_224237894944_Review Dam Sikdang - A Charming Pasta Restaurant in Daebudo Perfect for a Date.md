Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Last week, I visited Daebudo with my wife for a lunch date, choosing a pasta restaurant highly recommended on Naver blogs. It is not featured in Michelin guides or TV shows, but is well-known locally through word-of-mouth. I visited with modest expectations.

​

Upon arrival, the restaurant's exterior design immediately signaled that it would make a great destination for a date, regardless of how the food tasted.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAzMjdfMjYz/MDAxNzc0NTg1MTAxMTY3.yemUP4N0lF7TwpO6FT8yjjCDiVqo9LcziNUznuhDpUwg.pKrGYmsD5usw5cgzzWKxSj7FXSbYa3-Nbs3cH89LJxAg.JPEG/900%EF%BC%BF20260327%EF%BC%BF114345.jpg?type=w800)

​

It is situated in Daebudo, but in a slightly remote spot. Public transportation is not practical; driving is highly recommended.

​

The restaurant sits directly along the roadside, across from a gas station. It is not located next to the ocean or in a scenic area.

​

Knowing it opens at 11:30 AM, we arrived at 11:40 AM expecting to be seated immediately.

​

However, despite it being a Friday morning, we were placed on a waiting list.

We waited roughly 50 minutes. Once the first group of diners is seated at opening, a turnover cycle takes about 50 to 60 minutes, so expect a wait if you miss the first seating.

​

The wait raised our expectations. Stepping inside, the interior immediately confirmed that it is well-suited for dates.

​

The owner remodeled a traditional Hanok house, preserving the exposed roof beams while creating a clean, rustic interior.

​

Second is the Cheongyang Shrimp Cream Dam Pasta. I expected a spicy kick to balance the rich cream, but it carried almost no heat. Without the spice, the cream felt heavy.

​

Dam Sikdang's key strength is its excellent interior atmosphere. The pasta does not taste like instant noodles mixed with pre-made sauce; the recipes are properly prepared and taste good. While pricing is slightly high for the roadside location, it feels reasonable given the private room setting.

​

I rate this restaurant **4.1 out of 5 stars**. It is a recommended destination for couples and family dinners, rather than casual groups of friends.

​

Thank you.
