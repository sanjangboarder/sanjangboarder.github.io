Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

My youngest son requested tacos, so I review our visit to **Vanes Taco**. Please note that I do not typically seek out Mexican food, so this review reflects that perspective.

​

My first experience with Vanes Taco was at their Art Foret location near Songdo Central Park. It did not leave a strong impression on me at the time, but my two sons enjoyed the food. Since they wanted to return, we visited the Vanes Taco location at **Triple Street in Songdo, Incheon**. It operates as a local chain with two locations in Songdo, and the flavor profile is similar across both branches.

​

For dining districts in Songdo, Time Space and Triple Street are the primary options. While there is an older shopping district in District 1, Triple Street offers convenient parking and links directly to a large shopping mall, making it accessible.

​

The venue gets busy on weekends, often resulting in wait times. Since we live in Songdo, we visited on a weekday evening around 7:30 PM, and the dining room was relatively quiet.

---

​

Because Mexican cuisine is not my preference, my satisfaction was limited. However, since my children enjoyed the meal, the flavor profile appears correct. I prefer straightforward grilled meat options. Vanes Taco serves as a suitable option for families or couples, though it may be less appealing to groups of middle-aged diners seeking traditional Korean food.

​

I rate this establishment **3 out of 5 stars** based on my personal preference (*ajae palate*), though it warrants a **4 out of 5 stars** rating as a family dining option.
