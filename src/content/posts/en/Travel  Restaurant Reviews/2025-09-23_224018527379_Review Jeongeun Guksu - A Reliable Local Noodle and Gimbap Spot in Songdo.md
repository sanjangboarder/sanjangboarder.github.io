Last weekend, I wanted to have hot anchovy noodles and visited a local diner in Songdo. If you reside in Songdo and appreciate noodles and gimbap, you may already be familiar with this establishment. It is situated in the retail commercial arcade of the Hillstate mixed-use residential complex in District 1 (Block 4).

‚Äã

Since it is in a mixed-use complex, parking is available. The restaurant provides a two-hour parking validation. Although the overall block is busy, the parking area inside the residential sector remains manageable.

‚Äã

Visiting during dinner hours, I noted the exterior. It is a compact diner with a simple layout. The sign reads "Noodle & Gimbap", indicating its focus on standard snack-style menus.
