Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Although I reside in Songdo, Incheon, I visited the new Traders Wholesale Club store in Guewol-dong, which opened on September 5, 2025. It is reported to be one of the largest layouts in the country. I previously visited the Ansan and Songlim locations. While the Songlim location had layout limitations, the Ansan branch was spacious. I wanted to see how the Guewol branch compared. To avoid the opening day crowds, I planned my visit for Sunday evening.

​

The store is built on the site near the former agricultural produce market, and the structure is large.

​

Despite visiting late in the evening, I observed steady groups of shoppers entering the store.

​

Although there was no queue at the parking entrance, the crowd inside was substantial.
