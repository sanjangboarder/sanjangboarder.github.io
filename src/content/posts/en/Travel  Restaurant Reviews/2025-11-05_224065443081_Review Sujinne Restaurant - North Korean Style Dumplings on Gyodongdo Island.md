Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

A restaurant featured on MBC's TV show *Country Village Lee Jang-woo Season 2* caught my wife's attention. Since she enjoys dumplings and wanted to try their North Korean-style variety, we took a drive to Ganghwado Island to check it out.

​

The show depicted the food as highly appealing. Here is my review based on our visit.

​

Sujinne Restaurant is located near Daeryung Market on Gyodongdo Island.

​

Paid parking is available nearby, which is generally necessary on weekends. On weekdays, parking is occasionally available in front of the store, and a public parking lot is situated close by.

​

The restaurant appears to have been operating for a long time, though its popularity has increased since being featured on TV. We visited during a weekday lunch. While there was no line outside, the interior was full.

I was also interested to evaluate the North Korean-style dumplings themselves. The flavor was different from my expectations. These are kimchi dumplings, but instead of the typical tangy and spicy flavor, they carried a stronger scent of aged, fermented kimchi.

​

The dumplings were less satisfying for my preferences, though individuals who prefer the strong taste of fermented kimchi may evaluate them differently.

​

In summary, the side dishes at Sujinne Restaurant were of high quality. However, the broth of the hot pot was plain, and the dumplings were not to my preference. The overall portion size was generous, representing reasonable value. I would rate this restaurant 3.4 out of 5 stars. I would not choose to return if visiting Gyodongdo Daeryung Market again.

​

While it remains an option for those interested in trying this specific style of dumpling, it was less suited to my tastes.
