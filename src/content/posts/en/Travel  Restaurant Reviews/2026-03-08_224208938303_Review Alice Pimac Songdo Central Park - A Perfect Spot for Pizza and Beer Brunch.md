In Songdo, Incheon, there is a pizza and craft beer pub chain I have introduced before called **Alice Pimac**. Recently, they opened a new location across the street from Songdo Central Park. The venue was renovated with a "Day & Night" concept, offering a lunch menu, which I review below.

​

It is situated next to An's Bakery, which is one of the landmark bakeries near Songdo Central Park.
