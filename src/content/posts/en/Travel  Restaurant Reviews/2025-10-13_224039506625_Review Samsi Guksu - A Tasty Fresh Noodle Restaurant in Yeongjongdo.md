Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

During the recent holiday, I headed to Yeongjongdo Island to visit a steamed bun shop that was featured on the television show *Master of Life*. I tried calling ahead but could not reach them. Upon arrival, my suspicion was confirmed: the shop was crowded, and the steamed buns were already sold out. Since I was already there, I decided to dine nearby and walked into a restaurant, which turned out to be **Samsi Guksu**.

​

The location is detailed in the map below. It is located close to Haneul City and represents a convenient stop when traveling to the Manjeong or Jeongseong fishing ponds on Yeongjongdo Island.

​

The restaurant occupies an independent building. Samsi Guksu appears to be an independent local business rather than a franchise, specializing in noodles and suyuk (boiled pork slices).

The owner provided four slices of boiled pork (suyuk) as a complimentary side. The pork was tender.

​

While the cabbage kimchi was salty on its own, it paired effectively with the boiled pork slices, balancing the seasoning. I plan to order a full portion of suyuk on my next visit.

​

Samsi Guksu in Yeongjongdo served well-presented food. Although I visited without prior information, it was a successful discovery.

​

I would rate this restaurant **4.2 out of 5 stars**. It is a reliable option to recommend to visitors in the Yeongjongdo area. While popular tourist spots like Muuido Island, Eurwangri Beach, and Masian Beach are often crowded, this location offers a quieter dining environment.

​

I plan to visit again during future fishing trips to Yeongjongdo to try the noodles with a full portion of boiled pork. Anglers should note that the double portion is large and should be ordered accordingly.
