Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Today, I introduce a coffee spot recommended by a colleague. My office is located in Magok, Seoul, and a colleague who commutes from Songdo, Incheon, shared this recommendation. His wife visits this cafe regularly, and having visited it myself, I found it worth sharing.

​

The cafe is located at the back of the Prugio City shopping complex near the Art Center end of Songdo Central Park. Because the storefront is compact and situated away from the main road, it can be hard to spot.

​

While I frequently walk through this neighborhood for exercise, I had not noticed this shop before. Knowing its reputation for coffee, I examined the storefront, which has a modern feel. The name "Four Inch Tiger" is unique.

---

​

While Four Inch Tiger Coffee is located near Central Park, its position inside the shopping complex backstreets means it primarily serves local residents. As a Songdo resident, I had overlooked it. However, it is a hidden gem cafe worth visiting, and I plan to return. If you visit Central Park, this spot and **Luen Coffee** (located on the second floor of the nearby Art Foret complex) are recommended options.
