Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

In this post, I introduce a restaurant in Magok. It is a premium beef dining spot specializing in Hanwoo (Korean beef).

​

Refer to the map location below. It is located near Magok Station, within walking distance from Balsan Station.

​

The shop is on the second floor of Botanic Park Tower II. It is situated in a slightly recessed corner of the building, making it less visible from the street exterior.

Upon entering, the interior transitions to a refined, quiet atmosphere. The decor is structured for a formal dinner, making it suitable for hosting guests or business meals.

Some premium beef restaurants focus on a wide variety of side dishes (*Banchan*), while others concentrate on the beef. This venue focuses on the beef quality. The basic sides and sauces are neat, but the variety is limited to standard accompaniments.

​

Only a few side items are served to pair directly with the meat. These items are refilled upon request.

​

​

The mustard leaf kimchi (*Gat-kimchi*), mashed potatoes, and green onion kimchi were standard. The restaurant does not place heavy emphasis on side variety. A seasoned, chopped mushroom sauce was flavorful, but I encountered the identical product at a pork restaurant the following day, suggesting it is a commercial sauce rather than a house recipe.

​

For salts, one was a bokbunja (Korean black raspberry) salt. The quality of the salt was good.

​

We ordered the **4-Person Set A**. The price is 300,000 KRW, which averages to 75,000 KRW per 200g serving. While ordering the set is slightly more economical than separate cuts, the absolute price point is on the higher end for beef.

​

The quantity is moderate, and may feel insufficient for four adult men. To secure a full portion, ordering two sets is realistic; my party ordered twice to complete the meal.

---

​

Sogam Magok provides quality Hanwoo beef. Although the price point is high, the beef itself is prepared well. The side dish variety is a minor limitation. I rate this restaurant **3.7 out of 5 stars**. The cost makes regular visits difficult, but it is a viable option for hosting guests or business meals.
