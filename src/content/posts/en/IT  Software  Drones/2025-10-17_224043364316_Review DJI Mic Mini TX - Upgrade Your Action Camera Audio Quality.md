Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Although some time has passed since I purchased this item, I am now writing the review. This wireless microphone is designed to pair effectively with the DJI ActionCam series, which I use as my primary camera. Although the DJI Mic 3 was recently released at a higher price point, the Mic Mini remains a competitive option in terms of cost-effectiveness.

​

DJI holds a leading position in the consumer drone market and has established a strong presence in the action camera segment. Having used several action cameras, I find DJI's ecosystem to be reliable.

​

Previously, I mounted my action camera on a chest harness and recorded audio using the internal microphone, as high-fidelity audio was not a priority. However, when recording from a third-person perspective, my voice was often muffled or lost. I purchased this microphone to address that issue, and partly out of curiosity.

​

The transmitter and receiver (TX + RX) package is priced slightly above 50,000 KRW. If you intend to use it solely with compatible DJI action cameras, you only need to purchase the transmitter (TX), which costs under 50,000 KRW. Since I did not plan to record with a smartphone, I purchased the TX unit only.

​

The DJI Mic Mini can connect directly to smartphones via Bluetooth, though the audio quality is relatively low in this configuration. Purchasing the receiver (RX) is recommended for smartphone recording to secure better audio fidelity.

​

The package I received contained only the TX unit, packaged securely.
