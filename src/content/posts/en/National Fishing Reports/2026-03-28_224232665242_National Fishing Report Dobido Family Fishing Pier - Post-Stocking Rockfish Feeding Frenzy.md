Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

With members of TEAM MJT, our recreational fishing club, I visited the **Family Floating Fishing Pier** in Dobido, Dangjin, Chungnam for our March official outing. Because it had been a while since my last floating pier session, I prepared my gear thoroughly and arrived early.

​

However, we met with heavy sea fog. Passenger boats typically depart around 7:00 AM, but the seasonal temperature variance produced exceptionally thick sea fog. For safety, boat operations were suspended until 10:00 AM, when the fog finally began to lift.

​

​

Anglers board the ferry to the Family Floating Pier from a boarding platform near the Dobido ferry terminal. Our outing group consisted of 35 members, which is a larger group size compared to standard boat trips. The wait in the fog was tedious, but boarding the ferry raised everyone's spirits.

​

We boarded the ferry in groups of nine. The ferry was busy, reflecting the pier's reputation for high rockfish catch rates.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjA5MjhfMTQx/MDAxNzc0NjkwODAwMjE1._oD3L_auoOWwrbLpavVu1gam5X6OF5sbQzFU48lP1g4g.8MvtW9FNzOTdTgWbUPTCnGxDWMOVH5ZUmrQhfK2w5XQg.JPEG/900%EF%BC%BF20260328%EF%BC%BF095624.jpg?type=w800)

​

The ferry ride from Dobido Port to the floating pier is short, taking only a few minutes. We arrived safely.

​

The Family Floating Pier stocks a substantial volume of rockfish. They typically stock the ponds three times at one-hour intervals, though because of our delayed arrival, they ran the stocking windows at roughly 30-minute intervals. During the first window, a large volume of rockfish was stocked into the ponds.

​

​

I fished a compact telescopic stream rod purchased from AliExpress, which is a convenient setup for floating pier environments where short rods are preferred. Being telescopic, I rigged it at home, allowing for a 30-second setup on deck.

​

> **Dobido Floating Pier Tackle Setup**
> *   **Rod:** Purelure 1.5m Telescopic BFS Rod
> *   **Reel:** Banax Volante 2000
> *   **Line:** Shimizu Ultra-8 PE 1.0
> *   **Float:** Zzis Vertical Float / Zzis RB Float (Sizes 1.0 & 2.0)
> *   **Bait:** Live Smelt, Dyed Squid Strips
>
> *SanjangBorder*

​

I tested new stick floats from Zzis, which performed well. I paired them with my Banax Volante reel, which features a pleasant drag sound and reliable performance well-suited for pier fishing.

​

I landed two rockfish during the first stocking window, and the second window followed quickly. Additional rockfish were stocked into the ponds.

​

The post-stocking bite was active; nearly every member landed at least one fish, and experienced anglers caught three or four.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAzMjhfOTYg/MDAxNzc0Njk2MDUxODU4.49phng626_HdnXxWHn--lzoG2caNUfYDUWK-5t_f1kIg.gER9X1QOtXm6p19Ij1gS1bIZYfSe3RK3tvrZV2cJtlYg.JPEG/SE-13572e56-2a8a-11f1-b692-95ee72d64feb.jpg?type=w800)

​

The bite slowed once the stocking window closed. Because fish are most active within 20 minutes of stocking, capitalizing on this window was critical.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAzMjhfNDZf/MDAxNzc0NjkwNzk2NTM1.wLf4SF475j8PH49bC2cHwR7yMgSsdKKovLFKUymquZQg.6l_kgepj4gKKPlGbzc7HIAfey0ZQGevjDZ-vy8TtNh0g.JPEG/900%EF%BC%BF20260328%EF%BC%BF122135.jpg?type=w800)

​

I accumulated a respectable catch by the third stocking window. However, my photo did not turn out well.

​

I was focused on rigging and casting, preventing me from taking quality photos of the catch. Two young anglers in our group caught five rockfish each, and several first-time members landed two to four fish. Floating piers are excellent spots for beginners to experience fish action.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAzMjhfNDVf/MDAxNzc0Njk2MDYzMDI3.Aws2Zxq1Itgk2_6tTKC8vnQft9BvO7zD97cKxCKh47kg.Suq35rYNlmqh7p6qw5Q0748pmzHmGetG7V5vl1G1ELwg.JPEG/SE-1358b4f8-2a8a-11f1-b692-870a0ce2ec76.jpg?type=w800)

After the third stocking window, we had a late lunch. A local Chinese restaurant delivers orders to the Dobido ferry terminal, and we collected our food via boat, which was convenient.

We spent time talking and resting. Floating pier trips offer more opportunities for conversation among members compared to boat outings, which is a key benefit.

​

Several members donated giveaway items for the trip. Startech donated five pairs of quality 3-cut fishing gloves, I prepared egg jig sets, Dajabung donated a large batch of fishing towels, Potato donated a premium toothbrush sterilizer, and Poti provided cosmetic face masks, ensuring every member received a souvenir.
