Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

This year's monster flatfish (*flounder*) season started early. In a change from previous seasons, Incheon charters have reported stronger early results compared to the Chungnam area, but I had already booked several trips in Chungnam. To adjust my expectations, I joined a casual club outing with TEAM MJT members out of Namdang Port, boarding the **Cheonseongho**.

​

Namdang Port is a large, comfortable harbor with convenient parking. While it is less known than other hubs, it holds productive charter operations like the Cheonseongho.

​

I boarded the Cheonseongho last season. The skipper is helpful, running a continuous briefing on boat speed, current direction, weather, and depth profiles, which simplifies matching the bottom structure. Flatfish downshotting can become repetitive, but the continuous updates on depth and drift speed help identify focus zones.

​

We took a commemorative photo with the TEAM MJT members before departure. We departed around 5:30 AM.

I used JS Company Liberty Ocean rods in 3-power and 4-power. These rods offer good sensitivity and value, so I rigged a pair for this trip.

> *   **Setup 2:** JS Company Liberty Ocean B61-4 Rod | Daiwa Rise 100B Reel | Haedong LIMIT X9 PE 1.2 Braided Line
> *   **Rigs:** Tulbo Fishing Deli-rig Lux Straight Hook 4/0
> *   **Worms:** Rapala Trigger X Minnow (TB, PP, PK, WH patterns), Berkley HD
