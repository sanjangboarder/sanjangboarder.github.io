Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

The 2026 Korea International Fishing Show (KOFISH) has been announced. Online visitor pre-registration is open, and I am sharing the details below.

​

In South Korea, there are three primary fishing exhibitions held annually: KOFISH and the Korea Recreational Fishing Show at KINTEX in Ilsan, and the Incheon Fishing Show at Songdo Convensia in Incheon. Among these, KOFISH is the largest in scale.

​

It serves as the premier fishing exhibition in the country. For anglers interested in sea, freshwater, or boat fishing, it is a recommended event to visit.

​

---

​

In 2026, the event will take place at **KINTEX in Ilsan (Exhibition Center 1, Hall 2)**.

​

The show is scheduled from **Friday, March 6 to Sunday, March 8**, and will be co-located with the **Gyeonggi International Boat Show (KIBS 2026)**.

​

Admission is normally 10,000 KRW, but you can secure free entry by using the pre-registration system.

​

​

If you pre-register by **February 15**, admission is free. Under the updated policy for this year, registrations made after this date only receive a 50% discount, making early registration recommended.

​

There is no penalty if you register and do not attend, so I recommend securing your ticket early if you plan to go.

​

The primary focus of KOFISH is fishing tackle. It features the largest gather of tackle manufacturers in the country, including major brands like Shimano, Daiwa, N.S, and Haedong Tackle. Many of these brands do not participate in the other two local exhibitions.

​

In addition to tackle, the show features booths for camping, outdoor recreation, regional marine tourism promotions, and conservation efforts. Various interactive events are held daily.
