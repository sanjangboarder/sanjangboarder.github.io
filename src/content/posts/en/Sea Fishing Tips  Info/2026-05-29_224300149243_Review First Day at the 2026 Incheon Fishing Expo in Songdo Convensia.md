Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Since today was my day off, I decided to take a stroll over to Songdo Convensia—just a 40-minute walk or a 10-minute drive from my house—to visit the Incheon Fishing Expo. While the expo typically opens in August ahead of the webfoot squid (jjo-kko-mi) season to allow for quick egi shopping, this year it held its run in May.

​

Perhaps due to limited promotion and fewer participating brands, the expo hall felt somewhat quiet.

The show was smaller than last year, and a 30-minute walk was enough to explore the entire hall. I spent about an hour at the show, grabbing some ice cream and chatting with exhibitors. As a Songdo local, I hope the Incheon Fishing Expo draws more exhibitors and expands in the future.

​

If you plan to travel several hours to Songdo solely to visit the Incheon Fishing Expo, I wouldn't recommend it. However, if you combine it with exploring Central Park and visiting local restaurants for a weekend outing, it promises to be a satisfying trip.
