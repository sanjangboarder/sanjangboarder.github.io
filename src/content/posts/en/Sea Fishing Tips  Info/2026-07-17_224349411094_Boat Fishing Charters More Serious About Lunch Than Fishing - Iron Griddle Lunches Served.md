Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Last month, I attended the AOMEX Flounder Downshot Tournament and rode the Superman of the Team Luffy fleet. The lunch served there completely shocked me. Previously, I didn't care much about lunch on boat trips—I was highly satisfied with decent lunch boxes or typical Korean buffet-style meals.

​

But after having a pork belly lunch grilled on an iron plate, well... my standards have risen significantly. In this extreme summer heat, it might seem a bit over-the-top, but shouldn't we eat even better when it's hot?

​

So, I searched all over the West Sea charters—from Incheon to Chungnam and Gunsan—to investigate and organize a list of boat fishing charters that are serious about lunch and serve griddle-cooked meals. This is based on the situation up until last month, so it might vary slightly during midsummer, but it will serve as a great reference.

​

**Incheon Area**

In the Incheon area, there doesn't seem to be any place that serves a griddle lunch on the scale of the Team Luffy fleet. However, there are charters equipped for griddle lunches: Supernova out of Incheon Namhang and Lucina out of Yeonan Pier.

<div class="map-card glass"><div class="map-info"><span class="map-icon">📍</span><div class="map-text"><div class="map-name">Namhang Leisure Fishing Boat Pier</div><div class="map-addr">142 Chukhang-daero, Jemulpo-gu, Incheon</div></div></div><a href="https://map.naver.com/p/search/Namhang Leisure Fishing Boat Pier" target="_blank" class="map-btn">View Map</a></div>

Supernova out of Incheon Namhang is a very famous charter with many loyal fans, making reservations extremely difficult, so I haven't had the chance to ride it yet. According to my search, they serve stir-fried spicy pork (Duruchigi) cooked on a griddle. While it feels a bit modest compared to the Team Luffy fleet, it still stands out from other charters.

Another one is Lucina out of Yeonan Pier. Recently, they seem to be serving lunch boxes. I'm not sure if this is due to the extreme heat wave or the difficulty of preparing lunches on board, but they are equipped for griddle meals. When they did serve griddle lunches, they apparently served Dak-galbi (spicy stir-fried chicken).

**Chungnam Area**

​

The next charter I found is Eun-gabi out of Namdang Port. I didn't know because I hadn't paid attention, but there was a charter that served such amazing food at Namdang Port. Just looking at it makes my mouth water.

<div class="map-card glass"><div class="map-info"><span class="map-icon">📍</span><div class="map-text"><div class="map-name">Namdang Port</div><div class="map-addr">Namdang-ri, Seobu-myeon, Hongseong-gun, Chungcheongnam-do</div></div></div><a href="https://map.naver.com/p/search/Namdang Port" target="_blank" class="map-btn">View Map</a></div>

They do a proper pork belly griddle barbecue. They even grill kimchi in the rendered lard, so this is a menu that is virtually impossible not to taste amazing.

Lastly, the Team Luffy fleet out of Daecheon Port, which truly impressed me. The Team Luffy fleet operates three boats:

Luffy, Team Luffy, and Superman, and their lunches all seem to be identical. The style is similar to Eun-gabi of Namdang Port, but you could say it's even more upgraded. They include sausages and even finish with fried rice. Wow...

I couldn't find any charters serving griddle lunches in the Gyeonggi area, and though I searched through Gunsan and Mokpo as well, I couldn't find any others with my search skills. The charters introduced in this post might change their lunch offerings depending on their circumstances, but as of my check in June, Eun-gabi of Namdang Port and the Team Luffy fleet of Daecheon Port were definitely serving them, so please keep that in mind.

​

If you want to truly enjoy your lunch on board, booking a trip with one of these boats will certainly be a unique experience.
