Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Yesterday, I visited the Korea International Fishing Show, **KOFISH 2026**. Having attended this exhibition annually for several years, the venue feels familiar, yet I was curious to discover what new tackle gear and technologies would be unveiled this season. Although my initial expectations were modest, I encountered several interesting developments.

​

This year at KINTEX in Ilsan, parking near Exhibition Hall 1 was somewhat restricted due to construction, requiring visitors to park further away. However, since the complex is spacious, checking online for real-time parking space availability can help.

​

Despite a sudden drop in temperature, the weather was clear.

The new **Vanquish CE** spinning reel was available to test. Offered in sizes from 1000 to 2500, I tested the reel's winding action. Compared to my current 2023 Vanquish 2500, the 2026 Vanquish CE features reduced start-up inertia. The difference is subtle but noticeable in hand. The release is projected for April or May.
