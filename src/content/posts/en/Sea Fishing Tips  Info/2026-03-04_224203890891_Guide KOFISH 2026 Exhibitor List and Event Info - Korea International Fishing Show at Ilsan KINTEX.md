Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

The opening is tomorrow. The largest domestic fishing exhibition, **KOFISH 2026**, will be held at Ilsan KINTEX starting this Friday, March 6, for a three-day run. In the Seoul metropolitan area, three fishing shows are held annually: two at Ilsan KINTEX and one at Songdo Convensia. Among these, the Korea International Fishing Show (KOFISH) offers the most substantial product exhibitions.

​

Having attended all three events for several years, I noted that the 2025 International Fishing Show featured the most diverse exhibitor presence. With high expectations for the 2026 edition, I compiled the participant list and floor layouts.

​

Below is the exhibitor list and floor layout plan for KOFISH 2026.

​

The primary exhibits are arranged by major brands: **Shimano, AOMEX, Daiwa Korea, Haedong (HDF), and N.S.** The scale of their booths indicates the focus these brands place on the KOFISH event. Notably, these five major manufacturers do not participate in the other two smaller regional shows. This means KOFISH represents the only opportunity to review their complete lineups in person.

#### DAIWA

Daiwa Korea manages the largest booth footprint. While they focus on product exhibition rather than direct sales, visitors can purchase gear through their retail distributor, Pro Fishing, which exhibits nearby. However, reviewing the main Daiwa Korea displays is generally sufficient for most visitors.
