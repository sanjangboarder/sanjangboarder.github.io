**1. 2026 Shimano Spinning Reel Market Trends and Key Changes**

In 2026, Shimano is highlighting '**INFINITYCONNECT**' and **'The Pursuit of Essence'** as the core points of its brand. It seems their aim is to organically connect three elements—reel, rod, and gear (line)—beyond a simple combination of parts, thereby creating synergy as a 'systematic product.'

​

**Key Technical Achievements:**

*   Dramatic Performance Improvement: With the application of 'INFINITYCONNECT' specifications, Shimano states that reel strength has improved by approximately 25%, drag durability by more than 10 times, and initial power retention by 20% compared to previous models.
*   Diversification of Lineup: The launch of the Vanquish CE (Vanquish CE), the pinnacle of the MGL series, and the 26 EXSENCE, a flagship model exclusively for sea bass, has strengthened the high-end market lineup.
*   Trickle-down Technology: Core technologies from higher-end models have been incorporated into entry-level models such as the 26 Nasci and 26 Nexave, upgrading the entry class.

​

**2. Analysis of Shimano's Two Main Series: CORE SOLID vs MGL (Quick Response)**

Shimano's spinning reels are clearly divided into 'CORE SOLID' and 'MGL' series based on their design philosophy. Users should choose the optimal series according to their fishing style and frequency of operation. A summary of their simple features is presented in the table below.

| | | |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Features** | **CORE SOLID** | **MGL** **(Magnum Light / Quick Response)** |
| **Slogan** | Power and inertia, Substantial Strength and Robustness | Sensitivity and immediate response, lightweight |
| **Main Material** | Oriented towards full metal body and metal rotor | CI4+ (carbon resin) body and rotor applied |
| **Reeling Feel** | Heavy and smooth continuous reeling feel | Light and nimble initial reeling and immediate response |
| **Suitable Genres** | Walking lure, controlling large fish, float fishing | Surf lure, eging, delicate lure action |
| **Key Technologies** | Infinity Drive, Cross, Loop | Magnum Light Rotor, CI4+ ultra-light design |

The CORE SOLID concept provides overwhelming rigidity and smooth line release performance through 'Infinity Cross,' which maximizes gear durability, and 'Infinity Loop,' which spools the line in ultra-dense wraps.

**Considering purchasing a new 2026 spinning reel?**

From a cost-performance perspective, due to the current upward standardization of Shimano's technology, mid-range models like the Ultegra (late 100,000 won range) or Stradic and Vanford (200,000 won range) can provide ample performance for most fishing environments. I believe this is the most reasonable price range. They offer sufficient performance to serve as a main reel for over 3 years without needing an upgrade.

​

And if we consider the type based on genre, if you mainly do surf lure fishing (for flatfish, sand lance, etc.), the Quick Response series would be good. If you do walking lure fishing (for sea bass, large target fish), you should consider the Core Solid series. If eging (for bigfin squid, swordtip squid, etc.) is your main lure fishing, it would be beneficial to choose a Quick Response (MGL) series reel that is advantageous for sharp jigging actions and delicate drag adjustments.

**​**

For those new to fishing, choosing the Ultegra (mid-range performance) or the 26 Nasci (best cost-performance) rather than excessively cheap models is a choice that can prevent redundant investment 1-2 years later.
