â€‹

In cordless vacuums, suction power remains a primary parameter. The manufacturer specifications list a suction power of 18,000Pa at 110W. High-end robotic vacuum cleaners typically output around 20,000Pa, indicating that this compact unit offers comparable suction performance.

â€‹

The design includes a dual light system: a focus light and a mood light. These help illuminate dark areas during cleaning and add to the design. The unit weighs 410 grams, which is comparable to the weight of two smartphones, supporting comfortable handling.
