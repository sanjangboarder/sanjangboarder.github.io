Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Due to the volume of gear I carry for fishing and car camping, I selected the Hyundai Santa Fe MX5 when purchasing a vehicle last year. In under two years, I have logged over 40,000 kilometers. While the SUV offers a large cargo area, the 6-seater configuration presents a trade-off: when the 3rd-row seats are up to accommodate five or more passengers, the remaining trunk space is limited.

​

To address this limitation, I acquired a customized accessory. The Santa Fe MX5 features fixed window panels in the 3rd row, and this product is designed to utilize that space as storage organizer compartments. It is named the **IOM Santa Fe MX5 3rd Row Window Bag**.

​

The delivery arrived within one day. The package volume was relatively large. The product was protected by double packaging, and it includes two bags, one for each side of the vehicle.
