Hello, this is Sanjangboarder.

Daytime temperatures are already soaring past 30 degrees Celsius, bringing with them the scorching heat. Summer outdoor activities like camping, fishing, or even just walking to and from work can leave you drenched in sweat – that tough time of year is here again. Every year, we hear news of record-breaking heat, so I've been preparing various cooling items to beat the summer heat, and one of them is a neck cooler. They've been popular for a few years, and I've tried several products. However, some melt too quickly or have severe condensation, leaving my clothes wet and making me feel even more uncomfortable instead of cooler. Another type, the ice scarf, is always damp, which is a bit of a hygiene concern. And regular ring-shaped ice neckbands don't have enough contact area with the neck, so I've been looking for other options.

That's when I discovered a new neck cooler. Let's take a closer look at the 'Benight Wide Neck Cooler' released by Benight, a brand specializing in seasonal products. I was curious about this product, which features a partitioned style rather than the conventional ring shape.

Cooling the Benight Neck Cooler is incredibly simple. It has the characteristic of self-freezing in temperatures below 18 degrees Celsius, allowing it to be frozen in various ways, not just in a freezer. When immersed in ice water, it solidifies in about 10 minutes. It will also automatically start freezing if simply submerged in water below 18 degrees Celsius, such as cold water from a purifier or stream water.

The easiest way to freeze it solid is by placing it in a freezer, as shown in the picture below. I checked, and it was perfectly frozen in just 15 minutes.
