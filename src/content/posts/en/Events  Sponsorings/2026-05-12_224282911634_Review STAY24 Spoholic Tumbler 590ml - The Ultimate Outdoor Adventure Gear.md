With the weather gradually warming up, it is time to pack ice water and cold beverages for my fishing trips. While I often pack frozen water bottles in my cooler, this year I wanted to leave the heavy cooler in the car and carry a lightweight, well-insulated tumbler when hiking to fishing spots or boarding charter boats.

​

To find a lightweight, nicely designed tumbler with an appropriate capacity, I browsed the selection on STAY24. They offer a wide variety of styles and sizes at the store linked below:
