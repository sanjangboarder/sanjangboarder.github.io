Operation yielded a clean windshield. The wiping performance was more consistent than the factory blades.

​

The silicone blades are designed to offer temperature resistance, and the frame design helps balance the pressure applied by the wiper arms. This setup resulted in quiet operation and reduced noise compared to the old blades.

​

The water-repellent properties cleared residual moisture and streaks, confirming the performance of the silicone formulation.
