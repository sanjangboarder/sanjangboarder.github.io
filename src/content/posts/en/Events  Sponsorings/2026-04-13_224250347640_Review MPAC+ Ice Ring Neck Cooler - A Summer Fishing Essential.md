Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

While it felt chilly up until last week, the rising temperatures suggest outdoor activities will become hot starting in May. Since I fish frequently, I have been preparing summer essentials.

​

Personally, while sun protection hats and neck buffs are important, the most critical gear for hot weather is a neck cooler (also called an ice ring). Since these products hit the market a few years ago, they have become essential for surviving hot summers.

​

Last year, I accidentally dropped one of my neck coolers into the sea during a boat trip. To replace it, I purchased the **MPAC+ Ice Ring**. A unique feature of the MPAC+ line is the addition of a new "Mega" size in their 2026 lineup. For larger anglers like me who require larger sizes in hats, gloves, and buffs, the Mega size is a welcome option.

​

It arrived the day after ordering. The packaging was neat and secure.

The official model name is the **MPAC+ Neck Cooling Tube Ice Ring**. It utilizes a specialized PCM material that solidifies below 18°C. It freezes in just 15 minutes in ice water or 25 minutes in a standard freezer. It is proudly made in Korea.

Placing it around your neck provides an instant, air-conditioned feel. The manufacturer specifies that it delivers cool relief for up to 210 minutes.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAzMzBfMjQ0/MDAxNzc0ODMzODEwMTQz.F-DbHon7HypCI_IeQoQ5lkAQA9s4_TgO-f-z-KWmETEg.YbgTCu_jvhLAwwSWTi01aR7wVcvDqjdqdkexmJTD8Xkg.JPEG/900%EF%BC%BF20260329%EF%BC%BF153032.jpg?type=w800)

​

Using it is simple. Leave it in the freezer for 25 minutes, and it solidifies completely. Because its freezing point is higher than water's, it will freeze even if placed inside a standard food cooler or refrigerator during outdoor trips.

​

Additionally, if ice water is available, submerging the ring solidifies it in just 15 minutes.

​
