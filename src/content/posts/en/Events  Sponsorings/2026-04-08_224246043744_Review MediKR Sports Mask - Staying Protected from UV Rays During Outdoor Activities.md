Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

As spring weather arrives, the sun is growing stronger, making UV protection essential. Spending a few hours under strong sunlight can cause sunburn and skin damage, making protection critical.

​

My primary outdoor activity is fishing. Whether boat fishing at sea or walking along shores, I always carry a face mask to block UV rays. Recently, I added the **MediKR Sports Mask** to my gear bag, and I will share my impressions below.

​

The package arrived in clean condition. Interestingly, purchasing the MediKR Sports Mask includes a pair of bonus sports sunglasses. I will detail both items below.

​

The packaging lists the core specifications. It features a certified **UPF 50+** protection rating, and is constructed from a cooling nylon and spandex composite fabric that provides a comfortable stretch and dries rapidly.
