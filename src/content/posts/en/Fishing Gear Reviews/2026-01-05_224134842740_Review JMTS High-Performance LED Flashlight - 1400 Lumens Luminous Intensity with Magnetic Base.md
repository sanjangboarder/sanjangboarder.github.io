Operation is straightforward. A single press turns the light on. Pressing the button again after a delay turns the light off, while sequential presses cycle through the three brightness levels and the strobe mode.

​

Pulling the flashlight head forward focuses the beam into a spotlight mode, while pushing it back returns it to a wide floodlight mode. The zoom function allows you to adjust the beam width and projection distance. Unlike budget LED flashlights that often display a flickering effect (*flicker*) in photographs, the output of the JMTS LED remained stable.
