Hello, this is Sanjang Border.

There are a few essential fishing items I always pack when heading out for boat fishing. Lately, with the heat, it's necessary to bring a hat, buff, arm sleeves, and sunglasses to deal with the season. But there's one thing you should always have, regardless of whether it's summer, spring, or fall: a fishing towel.

Typically, fishing towels are attached to inflatable life vests with a carabiner. While many use small kitchen-sized towels, I recently acquired a fishing towel from Haedong that's incredibly compact for storage and can be unfolded for use.

Here's a look at the product packaging. It's designed as a dedicated fishing towel, and its name is the Well-being Microfiber Fishing Towel. As shown in the picture below, it's made for excellent portability, storing compactly in a pouch and unfolding for use.
