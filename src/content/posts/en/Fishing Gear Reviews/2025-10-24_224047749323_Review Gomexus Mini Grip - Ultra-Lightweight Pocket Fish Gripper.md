Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Whether float fishing or lure fishing, a lip grip (fish gripper) is a necessary tool when targeting species with sharp teeth, such as Spanish mackerel, largehead hairtail, flatfish, red seabream, and striped beakperch. However, standard lip grips are generally large and heavy, making them inconvenient to carry during active fishing trips.

​

When going light with just a few metal jigs, carrying a bulky gripper is impractical. I looked for a compact, lightweight alternative. Gomexus is scheduled to release the **G-Gripper E1 (Pocket Fish Gripper)** in November, and I obtained a pre-release specimen to evaluate its performance.

​
