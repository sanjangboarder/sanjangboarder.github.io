Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

With the West Sea webfoot octopus boat fishing season starting, many anglers are preparing their tackle box setups. Last season, there was one particular jig that was difficult to source due to high demand: the **YL Fishing Special Ika Sutte Small S-13**.

​

YL Fishing jigs became popular primarily through word of mouth among anglers rather than active marketing campaigns. Searching for YL Fishing or Yeollin Fishing online will yield numerous user reviews. The manufacturer produces a variety of colors and body designs, but this specific model is my preferred choice for webfoot octopus.

​

It is important to note that this jig is optimized specifically for webfoot octopus rather than cuttlefish. It performs well during the early season. As mentioned, the Small S-13 model is effective.

​

During last year's challenging session in the Chungnam sector, when overall catches were low and many anglers struggled to get bites, this color produced steady strikes. This performance established my confidence in the model, prompting me to order more of this specific color for the current season.

​

The price is approximately 4,000 KRW on Coupang, though it occasionally sells for 3,500 KRW. Due to inventory shortages, I recommend purchasing them whenever they are available to avoid missing out during the peak season.

​

Since this is an established product, I will keep the review brief.

While it is difficult to determine how webfoot octopus perceive this specific glow pattern in deep water, the signature is different from copycat products. Standard copies often emit a uniform single-color glow, whereas this model features a multi-tone contrast. Jigs from the same manufacturer with different glow signatures do not see the same demand, suggesting the S-13 glow pattern plays a role.
