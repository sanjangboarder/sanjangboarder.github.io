During trout ice fishing, using a fish finder confirms whether fish are passing under your hole, which provides peace of mind. Locating spots with higher trout activity directly increases your catch rate.

​

As a tactical tip: trout tend to gather near the outer boundary fences of the festival grounds rather than the center. Arriving early to scan these edge zones with your fish finder will improve your results.

For smelt ice fishing, you will note many experienced anglers using portable sonars on the ice. Smelt fishing is conducted in deeper waters compared to trout, making it difficult to locate the specific water column where the fish are cruising. A fish finder addresses this challenge. Knowing the exact depth of the smelt school makes a major difference. If you fish for smelt regularly, this joint-purchase discount offers a good opportunity.
