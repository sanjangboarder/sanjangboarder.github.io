Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Of all the fishing accessories I have purchased over my years of fishing, scissors are definitely among the most frequent items. Being small, they are easily lost, and they frequently end up discarded once the blades grow dull. Because I set up separate tackle boxes, lure bags, and life jackets for different fishing genres, the number of scissors in my collection has grown steadily.

​

The scissors attached to the Haedong inflatable life jacket I wear most frequently were no longer cutting cleanly, so I acquired a pair of the new **Haedong Compact Braid Scissors**. Haedong recently released several new scissor models, and since I purchased a few, I will introduce the other models in future posts.

​

The Compact Braid Scissors are packaged as shown below. Scissor prices range widely from 1,000 KRW to models costing 20 to 30 times more. Budget products carry clear quality limitations. I recommend purchasing mid-range scissors priced above 5,000 KRW, as quality scales directly with price. The Haedong Compact Braid Scissors retail in the 8,000 to 9,000 KRW range on Naver Shopping, representing a highly capable, premium accessory.

While the tips are not sharp, the serrated blades cut cleanly and could potentially damage other tackle, apparel, or life jackets if left open. To prevent this, the scissors feature an integrated locking slider. You can slide the lock up or down easily to lock or open the blades.

The cutting edges feature micro-serrations rather than a smooth bevel. A serrated edge significantly improves cutting performance on braided lines. When blades dull even slightly, cutting PE braid without serrations often results in frayed ends. These scissors cut cleanly without fraying.

​

It also includes a hidden feature that is highly welcome for braid users: a line detangler pin. When line tangles occur, having a fine pin to insert into tight knots can save you massive frustration. If a tangle is too severe, you can cut it away instantly, but if it looks salvageable, you can pull the detangler pin out of the handle by rotating the base (indicated by the arrow below). Integrating this pin directly into the scissors is an excellent design detail.

​

As you fish longer, you naturally begin seeking higher-quality accessories. Scissors are an essential tool, and cheap pairs often rust, fail to cut cleanly, or lock up. If you are looking for a reliable, high-quality tool, the Haedong Compact Braid Scissors will prove highly satisfying.

​

I plan to use these primarily for boat fishing, but since compact accessories are ideal when rock fishing or shore casting, they are an excellent choice for walking setups as well.
