Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Recently, I added the **Danasea Keutpanwang Jjugab 158B** and **Baekgab 158B** rods from Fishing Korea to my setup. These models are configured for the early autumn webfoot octopus and cuttlefish boat seasons.

​

The series offers 8:2 and 9:1 action variants to match preferences. Since I prefer shorter rods for active jigging, I selected the 158cm configuration for both models.

​

Both rods are suited for webfoot octopus drifts and cuttlefish drifts. They are priced at approximately 50,000 KRW, positioning them as budget options. However, their weight and sensitivity characteristics compare well with higher-priced alternatives.

​
