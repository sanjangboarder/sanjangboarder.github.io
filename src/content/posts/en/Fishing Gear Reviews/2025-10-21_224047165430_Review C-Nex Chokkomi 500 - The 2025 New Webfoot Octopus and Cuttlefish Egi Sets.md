Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

‚Äã

Among domestic fishing gear brands, companies that operate their own factories to design and produce webfoot octopus and cuttlefish egis are relatively rare. C-Nex is well-known for its domestic design and manufacturing of the Chokkomi egi line. Despite the highly competitive market with diverse copycats, C-Nex has released the **2025 Chokkomi 500** series, prompting me to evaluate its new features.

‚Äã

The 2025 Chokkomi 500 is available in five set configurations. Let's examine them individually.

‚Äã

A notable feature of the C-Nex line is the sturdy individual plastic case packaging, which protects the hooks and bodies during transport and simplifies storage.
