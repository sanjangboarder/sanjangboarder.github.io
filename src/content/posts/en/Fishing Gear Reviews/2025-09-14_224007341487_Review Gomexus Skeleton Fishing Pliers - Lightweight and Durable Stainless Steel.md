Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

For anglers engaged in lure fishing, a reliable set of pliers represents an essential accessory. Pliers serve multiple roles on the water, from extracting hooks and opening split rings to cutting braided lines.

​

Recently, Gomexus introduced a new model: the **Gomexus Skeleton Fishing Pliers**.

​

Gomexus is recognized for producing well-designed, high-quality fishing gear, and this new model follows that pattern. Positioned as a flagship accessory, it is distinct from standard budget pliers. The care in design is evident from the packaging.

​

The packaging includes the pliers, a dedicated sheath, and a lanyard.

​

The pliers are constructed from **17-4PH stainless steel**, an alloy selected for its corrosion resistance in marine environments and its structural strength.

​

To further protect the alloy from saltwater exposure, the surface features a titanium coating.

​

<div class="single-image">
<img src="https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNTA5MTRfMTI3/MDAxNzU3ODE5MTA4NDUz.gKbCJH5gRGaGP_edDaqhnFPFGAN55kaElcXgke6HeGUg.M30WCddz8m-lKlEKIH2vnpjbwIrw63PZETTQNze09Oog.JPEG/SE-629d5039-8c59-11f0-b1e7-ffe53a7bb471.jpg?type=w800" alt="Pliers profile view" />

​

The manufacturer indicates that the alloy resists corrosion even after 168 hours of continuous saltwater immersion. While anglers rarely submerge tools for such durations, this highlights the material's durability. The body design features a central circular frame element that adds rigidity and gives the tool a distinct look.
