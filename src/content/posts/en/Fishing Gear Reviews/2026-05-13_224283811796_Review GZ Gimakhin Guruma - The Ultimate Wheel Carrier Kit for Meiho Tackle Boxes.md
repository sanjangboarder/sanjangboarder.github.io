Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

At the Korea International Fishing Show this year, I spotted a highly innovative accessory that I want to share. Found at the booth of creator Ganzi, it is the **GZ Gimakhin Guruma** (meaning "Amazing Cart/Wheels"). I was unaware of it initially, but discovered it is already retailing online.

​

Looking at the packaging, the box is compact and contains a set of wheels and bolts. As you can guess, this is an idea-driven accessory designed to attach wheels directly onto your tackle box. Specifically, it consists of custom parts that mount onto standard Meiho tackle boxes (as well as major copycat models). Since Meiho tackle boxes (and their generic equivalents) are exceptionally popular, this kit has wide compatibility.
