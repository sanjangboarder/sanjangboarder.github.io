Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

At the 2026 Korea International Fishing Show held at KINTEX, Ilsan, the booth that left the strongest impression on me was **AOMEX**.

​

While I fish most saltwater genres, my primary focus is casting lure and offshore boat fishing. AOMEX has updated their product line for these categories, and I have monitored their developments since last year. After trying out a rod developed in collaboration with fishing YouTuber *Blacky*, I recognized their engineering quality. Most of the 2026 models are scheduled for release around June, and the show provided a preview of the upcoming lineups.

### AOMEX Flagship Rod Series: Navigator

Detailed specifications for the Navigator series are not yet fully published. However, YouTuber *Blacky*, who participated in the development cycle, provided details at the show booth.

​

<div class="single-image">
<img src="https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAzMDhfMjI3/MDAxNzcyOTc0MTM4Njk1.jEDXcd27H3Jx0ZjrGMafxC_E-fZ7pFrG8VtaicHDaN4g.0SX21P1e97pG0UyJX-kgPs9we3Crx37Rg7QXFeeGldwg.PNG/image.png?type=w800" alt="Navigator logo" />
</div>

​

The estimated release price is approximately 380,000 KRW, matching its flagship position. The blanks incorporate three of AOMEX's core technologies: **PENTASTART Carbon Architecture** (a 5-way multi-axial structural stress configuration), the **HIGH BROAD System** (a next-generation full solid blank), and the **Adaptive Resonance Carbon Grip System**.

​

The blanks utilize Toray **T1100G** and **M40X** carbon fiber. These materials represent high-grade composites commonly reserved for premium lineups due to their strength-to-weight characteristics.

​

The Navigator series offers several specialized models. Two eging models are scheduled; the **83MLMS-Tb** stands out, measuring 2.52m and weighing 76g. The physical balance felt correct during hand tests at the booth.

​

Two lightweight tip-run models are also scheduled. Two inshore models, weighing between 100g and 110g, are designed to target species up to sea bass. These models are configured for spinning reel applications.

​

For offshore boat fishing, the series includes flatfish downshot and tai rubber models. A cuttlefish model was also displayed at the booth, though it was not yet listed on the website. These boat models are configured for baitcasting reels.

### AOMEX Mid-Range Rod Series: Newgate

The **Newgate** series consists of 8 models, focusing on offshore boat applications: downshot, tenbin, swordtip squid, octopus, and cuttlefish models. It also includes a model configured for paid fishing ponds.

​

The Newgate series utilizes **METALLICAL TITAN** (a metallic titanium direct-transmission structure), **Cross Reverse Technology** (crossed reverse structure control), and **Intelligent Curve Technology** (intelligent curvature control). These features are designed to provide durability and flex action uncommon in the mid-range bracket.

​

### AOMEX Entry-Level Rod Series: Exvia

Flagship and mid-range options can exceed the budget requirements of entry-level anglers. Entry-level models are typically designed with a heavier focus on durability to handle common handling errors. The **Exvia** series is configured to maintain durability while delivering functional casting and jigging performance.

​

<div class="single-image">
<img src="https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAzMDhfMjM5/MDAxNzcyOTc0MjUzNDc2.FMdFYYxE-lrdn3fwaev0uO7tdMPMbyc-JC9hiliSJcUg.9V22TP0WFBlsso52IW1t3mUCIYUFXUKpCHG0siOved8g.PNG/image.png?type=w800" alt="Exvia logo" />
</div>

​

The series is priced at 149,000 KRW, presenting a entry-level option. The Exvia, Newgate, and Navigator series share a consistent design theme, with differences lying primarily in material grades and component specifications.

​

The Exvia series consists of 4 models, focusing strictly on downshot jigging. A downshot spinning jigging model is included, which is less common in this segment. The suffixes "2F" and "4F" in the model names indicate power ratings. Entry-level anglers will find the stiffer 4F action practical for managing heavy rigs.
