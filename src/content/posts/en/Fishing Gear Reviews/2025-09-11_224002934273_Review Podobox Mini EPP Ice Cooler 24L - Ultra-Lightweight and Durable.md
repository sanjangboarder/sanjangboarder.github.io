Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

In this post, I introduce an ice cooler that matches the lightweight configuration I have been seeking. When evaluating a fishing cooler, thermal retention is often the primary criterion.

​

While thermal retention is important, a cooler that maintains cold conditions for 24 hours is typically sufficient for day trips. Anglers on boat or shore trips usually depart early in the morning and return late at night, rarely exceeding a 24-hour window.

​

If basic cold retention covers 24 hours, the weight of the cooler becomes the next significant factor for convenience. Standard 20 to 24-liter hard coolers weigh between 4kg and 6kg empty. While they offer extended insulation up to 72 hours, a lighter setup is more practical for single-day trips.

​

I sought a model that combines functional insulation with low weight, and located this model.

​

It is called the **Podobox EPP Cooler**. Let's check the details of this model.

​

Squid ink stains are inevitable during cuttlefish and octopus trips, but the black color hides them. The EPP coating allows you to spray down the surface with fresh water to clean it after use. The model drew interest from other anglers on the boat. I plan to use it as my primary cooler for upcoming octopus and cuttlefish drifts.

​

For those seeking to reduce tackle weight, this model is worth checking out.
