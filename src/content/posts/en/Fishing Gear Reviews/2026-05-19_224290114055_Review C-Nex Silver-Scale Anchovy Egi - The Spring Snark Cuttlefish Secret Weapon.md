Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Among squid jigs designed for cuttlefish last year, the model that caught my attention was the C-Nex Silver-Scale Anchovy. While the market is flooded with Chinese OEM jigs of similar designs, the domestic C-Nex Silver-Scale Anchovy features a new style. I had success with it during last autumn's cuttlefish season.

​

To replace the jigs I lost last season, I purchased a new batch of C-Nex Silver-Scale Anchovy jigs. Today, I share a detailed review of this lineup.

​

The two styles differ in their Keimura UV response. Over ten years of cuttlefish fishing, I have noted that cuttlefish do not always favor high Keimura intensity. Preparing a selection of low to high glow intensities is recommended.

​

The standard Silver-Scale Anchovy has a moderate Keimura response. The horse mackerel color has no Keimura response, while the pink and blue models show subtle glow.
