Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

â€‹

For several seasons, I have logged the transition toward lightweight reels and rods. I prefer lightweight tackle configurations. This webfoot octopus and cuttlefish season has seen multiple lightweight items introduced to the market. NH Korea is one brand contributing to this category. Last month, I utilized the NH Korea Dark Air 48g rod, which aligned with my preferred tackle setup.

â€‹

Recently, NH Korea introduced an ultra-lightweight baitcasting reel. While there is no official industry standard defining ultra-lightweight reels, the 2025 models indicate that a reel must weigh under 120 grams to enter this category.

â€‹

The new NH Korea **Core Speed Air** baitcasting reel weighs 103 grams. Two seasons ago, reels weighing under 120 grams were rare. Approaching 100 grams is a notable development. In this review, I will analyze the design features that enable this light weight.

â€‹

The reel is priced in the high 100,000 KRW range. Rather than a budget model, it is positioned as a specialized, ultra-lightweight reel. The packaging box is designed to reflect this positioning.
