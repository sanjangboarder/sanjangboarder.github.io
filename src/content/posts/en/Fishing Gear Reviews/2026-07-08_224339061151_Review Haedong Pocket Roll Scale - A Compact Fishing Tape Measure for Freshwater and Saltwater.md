Whether you are fishing in saltwater or freshwater, landing a decent-sized fish immediately calls for a measurement. Excluding massive trophy species, the majority of common catches fall under 50cm. Today, I'm reviewing a product designed specifically to measure these everyday catches: the **Haedong Pocket Roll Scale (Pocket Scale)**.

​

As you can see below, it features a roll design that makes it compact enough to fit inside your pocket. It is available in black, red, and green.
