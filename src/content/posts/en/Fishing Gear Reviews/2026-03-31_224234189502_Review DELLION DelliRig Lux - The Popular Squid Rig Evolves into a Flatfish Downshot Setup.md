​

DELLION by Tulbo Fishing, a popular brand for squid rigs, has released an innovative new product for the 2026 giant flatfish (halibut) season. I secured this new model ahead of release, and review its features below.

​

The packaging and shipping from Tulbo Fishing are neat. Downshot rigs (often called flatfish downshot rigs) utilize a leader line with a straight or wide-gap hook positioned above the sinker.

I received both the straight hook and wide-gap (offset) hook variations of this new flatfish downshot rig.

The model name is the **DELLIRIG LUX**. It combines the mechanics of the DelliRig sliding squid rigs—which saw heavy popularity over 2024 and 2025—with upgrades optimized for flatfish downshot applications.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjA0MDVfMjI1/MDAxNzc1ODM4MDg5NDM2.op1qvjCJdrHiSrTzLbdB5JgiIVvB91aWCAiFzWJvKwcg.t4SgedmR-yQ7NZsM-17xT50dg8ibbxuhynZ1F0yofgMg.JPEG/SE-5edc9cb6-29bd-11f1-87ec-ffc984a2dd06.jpg?type=w800)

​

Examining the straight hook model first: it features a 4/0 hook rigged on a 90cm, Size 5.0 (20LB) monofilament leader. While anglers typically use offset heights (distance between hook and sinker) of 30cm to 60cm, the 90cm leader length accommodates a wider range of adjustments.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjA0MDVfMjkz/MDAxNzc1ODM4MDA1NDQy.EUb-fSHMF3jnyymaFFY0ughmQvFV6VlzrFSWuClY76kg.k65mknhgxBO04fV0yI3YXmAMxT31HtaBVpZw1PHMOw8g.JPEG/SE-5edd6007-29bd-11f1-87ec-c5378649691f.jpg?type=w800)

The 20LB monofilament leader provides sufficient strength to land meter-class flatfish. Each pack contains 3 pre-rigged setups. For general trips, a single pack is sufficient, though bringing a backup pack is recommended if fishing over snaggy bottoms.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjA1MDVfNjQg/MDAxNzc1MzczOTEzMTY4.zUPQXg1aEhNGW5w5c6mKhJMV663EdV_iAzz7uPVNFXcg.jMrh2S5ZJqwkWqAIFdsPxffSKfeIDWMvYHn7oC_kyN0g.JPEG/900%EF%BC%BF20260327%EF%BC%BF093333.jpg?type=w800)

The rig unravels easily by untying the gold wire strap.

The primary innovation of the DelliRig Lux is the patented **ABS adjustable height system**. As shown below, the hook is mounted to a black collar tube, positioned between two sliding knots similar to those on their squid rigs.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjA1MDVfNDgg/MDAxNzc1MzczOTExOTU0.EemcXRhLMjVyvI_NyPmbFX6ZPdkeRM-VjgXZduwwPd8g.LnvPUtIelXeXskWOamvDSuMzDXhEv4e_DWLXnF54PQMg.JPEG/900%EF%BC%BF20260327%EF%BC%BF093720.jpg?type=w800)

​

The leader line terminates in a top swivel that can be tied to the braided main line, or removed to tie the braid directly to the mono leader. The bottom features a robust snap-lock swivel to keep the sinker secure.

The adjustable height system is straightforward. Releasing the sliding knots on the leader line enters height-adjustment mode, while sliding the knots together locks the hook position.

​

![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjA0MDVfMTE5/MDAxNzc1ODM4NDEyNDc0.5cEfOOIF48ihupttLUDHXXmST7Vgfp0R6FX5whUyakIg.mGGRu3Z_ONJnLYiy-xAHGurwNQ1WBb0InPKfVCzECvMg.JPEG/SE-5eee01ea-29bd-11f1-87ec-830917893efd.jpg?type=w800)
![](https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjA0MDVfMjI4/MDAxNzc1ODM4NDIyMTY4.nhIatUCgSsTX_MFdDCciqBI8dB5bxuASnKyPoUhlRFwg.QTu8kQumXjZSAjlDvTSb85RNg0oTzsOdad8YHiLk2isg.JPEG/SE-5eec0617-29bd-11f1-87ec-6fe4ea36ff43.jpg?type=w800)

​

The knots utilize clear monofilament. Standard sliding downshot rigs use two beads to adjust height. While functional, beads create friction points that increase the risk of line breakage under the load of a large fish. Because of this risk, I avoid bead-based systems.

​

In contrast, the DelliRig Lux system secures the hook position without creating friction points on the leader, making it a reliable way to adjust hook height.

​

Additionally, the hook is removable. Instead of replacing the entire rig to change hook sizes or styles, the hook shank can be slid out from the black collar tube. This allow you to swap hooks quickly, and the design is patented.

​
