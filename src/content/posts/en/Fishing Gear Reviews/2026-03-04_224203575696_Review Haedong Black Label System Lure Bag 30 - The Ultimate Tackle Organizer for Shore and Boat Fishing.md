Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

I have previously introduced a fishing bag by Haedong that is practical for boat fishing. Today, I present a review of their smaller utility bag that follows a similar design concept: the **Black Label System Lure Bag 30**. I used it on my first shore casting trip to Seonjae Fishing Park this season, and it proved to be a practical gear setup.

​

---

​

HDF Haedong's packaging is clean. The bag arrived wrapped in protective vinyl inside a shipping box. My first impression was positive: the clean black color scheme combined with the carbon-patterned hard lid gives the bag a refined appearance.

The model designation is **HB-2553**, and the official name is the **BLACK LABEL System Lure Bag 30**. While labeled as a "System Lure Bag Size 30" targeting lure anglers, selection should depend on the volume of gear you intend to carry. For boat fishing sessions where you require minimal tackle, this bag is also sufficient.

​

The Haedong System Lure Utility Bag 30 serves as a viable alternative to hard tackle boxes, offering several storage advantages. Since using these soft utility bags, I rarely carry traditional tackle boxes. The main benefit is the built-in shoulder strap along with pre-mounted gear attachments, eliminating the need to purchase add-on accessories.

​

The bag is salt-resistant and durable, making it suitable for boat sessions as well as lure casting or iso float fishing from the shore. If you have only used traditional tackle boxes, this item is worth considering.
