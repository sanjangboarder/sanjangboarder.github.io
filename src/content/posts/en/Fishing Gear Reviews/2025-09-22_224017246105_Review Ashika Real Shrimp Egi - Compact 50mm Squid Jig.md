Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

This season has seen high interest in compact squid jigs (chokkomi egis) for webfoot octopus and cuttlefish. The Real Shrimp egi, which was popular last season, will be useful as the cuttlefish season starts. However, the standard Real Shrimp jigs are typically manufactured in 80mm or 60mm sizes. These larger dimensions made them less suitable for the early cuttlefish season or for targeting webfoot octopus specifically.

​

Recently, a smaller version was introduced: the **Ashika Real Shrimp Egi**. While designed primarily for cuttlefish, these shrimp-pattern jigs are applicable for targeting webfoot octopus, swordtip squid, common octopus, and bigfin reef squid. Their primary applications remain cuttlefish and webfoot octopus.

​

The order arrived quickly. I purchased a set of the Ashika Real Shrimp jigs in multiple colors. They were packaged in a compact box.
