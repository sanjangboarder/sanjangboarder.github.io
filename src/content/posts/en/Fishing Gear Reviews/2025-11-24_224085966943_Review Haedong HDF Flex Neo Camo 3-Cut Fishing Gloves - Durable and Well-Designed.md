Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

Among domestic fishing tackle manufacturers, several brands produce specialized fishing gloves. One of the brands that consistently delivers good quality is Haedong Tackle (HDF). I recently purchased their newly released **Flex Neo Camo 3-Cut Fishing Gloves** to evaluate their design and durability.

​

I go on approximately 50 fishing trips a year. To prevent fish odor, I wash my fishing gloves after every trip, which reduces their overall lifespan. Fishing gloves are essentially consumables; after about 50 trips and subsequent washes, they wear down and develop holes. I had been looking for a pair with better durability, and HDF's Flex Neo Camo 3-Cut gloves seem designed to address this requirement, while featuring an appealing color scheme and design.

​

The packaging is labeled "FISHING APPAREL," indicating HDF's expanding focus on fishing garments. The gloves are available in two sizes; I selected the L/XL size to accommodate my larger hands. The package contains a single pair of gloves.

While previous HDF gloves featured printed logos, these gloves utilize a separate, stitched-on rubber logo patch. Printed logos tend to peel off over time, a problem that is avoided with this stitched patch. The finger joints feature slit cuts to improve finger mobility, indicating careful design.

​

These gloves are suitable as primary gear for spring, summer, and autumn, provided temperatures remain above freezing. When temperatures drop below freezing, switching to dedicated insulated winter gloves is recommended. This model is configured for early spring to late autumn, and is well-suited for walking lure fishing, boat fishing, and float fishing applications.
