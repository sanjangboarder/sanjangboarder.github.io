Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

ŌĆŗ

Recently, domestic icebox manufacturer Haedong introduced the **Carry Cooler** series. It is available in three sizes: 18L, 24L, and 30L. I acquired the 24L model, which represents the most versatile size in the lineup, to evaluate its features.

ŌĆŗ

The Haedong Karisma Cooler series is recognized among anglers and campers for its ice retention and durability. Many anglers rely on the Karisma series for both boat and shore sessions. While the build quality is high, these coolers have a notable disadvantage: they are heavy.

ŌĆŗ

To address this, the manufacturer could either reduce the core weight or make the unit easier to transport. Haedong addressed this by developing a carrier-style cooler with integrated wheels and a handle.

ŌĆŗ

The product arrived in a dedicated shipping carton. The packing was robust, utilizing double boxing to protect the unit during transit.
