### 5 Core Technologies of the Shimano Vanquish CE

​

#### 1. MGL (Magnumlite) Rotor: 28% Inertia Reduction

The first pillar defining the Vanquish's technical profile is the **Magnumlite Rotor**, representing the peak of the MGL series. Using an asymmetric design, it optimizes rotational balance and trims unnecessary mass, achieving a 28% reduction in rotational inertia compared to the 22 Stella (2500S size).

​

Lower inertia minimizes rotational resistance when starting and stopping the retrieve, improving control. The reel starts turning with minimal effort and stops immediately when force is removed.

​

This low-inertia response allows the angler to stop and start the lure instantly, transmitting subtle water tension variations to the hand as clean feedback.

​

Additionally, the Vanquish CE reduces rotational torque further by optimizing the internal waterproof seal placement.

#### 2. Infinity Loop (Super Slow Oscillation)

The Vanquish integrates the **Infinity Loop** oscillation system, which was previously exclusive to the Stella. By slowing the spool's vertical travel speed, the system aligns the line wraps close together on the spool. This organized alignment reduces line release resistance during casts, yielding a smooth casting feel.

​

Crucially, the low-inertia MGL rotor and vibration dampening of the Infinity Loop combine to eliminate mechanical noise. By removing minor oscillation stutters, only pure underwater signals are transmitted. This technical synergy produces the high level of sensitivity Shimano calls "mastering the water."

​

#### 3. Dura Cross Drag: 10x Wear Resistance

The concern that lightweight reels are less capable when fighting large fish is addressed by the **Dura Cross** drag washer. This technology uses a material innovation: weaving fibers perpendicular to the traditional pattern to maximize density and strength.

​

This material change achieves 10 times the wear resistance of conventional drag washers while maintaining smooth drag performance. Dura Cross provides substantial durability within the Vanquish's lightweight frame.

​

#### 4. Infinity Cross & Infinity Drive

The gear design incorporates **Infinity Cross** and **Infinity Drive** systems. Infinity Cross optimizes gear tooth contact, widening the surface area between the drive gear and pinion gear. Distributing load across a wider surface area improves gear durability by roughly two times.

​

#### 5. Anti-Twist Fin

The reel addresses line management through the **Anti-Twist Fin**, an elastic fin positioned near the line roller. This fin maintains light tension on the line as it feeds onto the spool, preventing line slack from causing loops or knots.

​

Line tangles disrupt fishing flow and rhythm, and the Anti-Twist Fin helps prevent these issues, allowing the angler to focus on fishing.

​
