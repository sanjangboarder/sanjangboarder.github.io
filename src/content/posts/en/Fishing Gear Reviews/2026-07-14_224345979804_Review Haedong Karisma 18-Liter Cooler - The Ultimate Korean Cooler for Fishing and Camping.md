Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

When the peak summer season arrives, the number one item you must pack for camping or fishing trips is a high-quality cooler. An ice box is not only essential for keeping various drinks cold and refreshing under the hot sun but is also critical for preserving caught fish in fresh condition.

​

Anglers have likely seen the Haedong Karisma cooler in action across many fishing fields. Haedong Karisma is a domestic Korean brand that offers what is practically considered the ultimate premium cooler. While it comes in various capacities, I acquired the highly practical 18-liter size to travel lighter this summer.

​

As always with Haedong tackle products, the shipping was fast and the packaging was extremely secure. The Haedong Limited Karisma 18-Liter product arrived cleanly packed inside double boxing with plastic wrapping.

I ran an actual insulation performance test: I placed a 2L water bottle, frozen for 2 days at -20°C, inside the cooler and left it on my veranda for 25 hours. After 25 hours, the ice was only 50% melted, and the interior of the cooler remained cold.

​

Last week, I went on a white croaker fishing trip and caught about 5kg of fish. I left home at 2 AM carrying a 2L ice water bottle inside the cooler. Despite fishing all day and opening the lid three times to drop fish inside, when I checked at 5 PM, the croakers were kept in extremely fresh condition, and half of the ice in the 2L bottle remained frozen. Even though I opened it more than 10 times to grab drinks throughout the 15 hours, it performed flawlessly.
