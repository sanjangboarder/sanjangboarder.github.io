Hello, this is Sanjang Boder.

It seems the boat fishing season has officially begun. While I still go shore fishing or visit fishing spots, boat fishing definitely accounts for over 50% of my trips now. And when I go boat fishing, there's a must-have accessory I always attach to my life vest, which is a crucial safety item.

Those who do a fair bit of boat fishing probably always have a braided line cutter or pliers attached to their inflatable life vest. I'm no different. Compact products with excellent cutting power are popular these days. Cheaper braided line cutters tend to be very susceptible to rust and lose their sharpness after a few uses, so you end up replacing them quickly. That's why I'd like to introduce a pretty good product.

It's a product called the COMPACT Multi-Pliers from Haedong. It's a new item that hasn't been out for long. I introduced a similar braided line cutter last month, but this time it's small pliers.

Naturally, like a braided line cutter, it has a serrated cutting blade to neatly cut lines, and it's a multi-purpose plier that can also tune small split rings needed for lure fishing.

While it functions as pliers and a line cutter, it also features a line tangle-release pin for added functionality. When line trouble occurs, nylon or fluorocarbon lines can often be disentangled relatively easily by hand, but with braided lines, you usually end up giving up. This is where having a line tangle-release pin makes a huge difference. Gently inserting the pin between the lines and easing the tension can be a great help in resolving tangles, making it a surprisingly useful feature.
