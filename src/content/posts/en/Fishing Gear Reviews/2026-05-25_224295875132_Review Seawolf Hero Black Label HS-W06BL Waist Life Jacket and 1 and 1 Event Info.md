Let's look closely at this automatic inflatable waist belt life jacket. Inflatable waist belt life jackets have gained massive popularity over the past few years, with various brands entering the market. Because safety is the absolute priority, you should verify if a model holds official **KOMSA (Korea Marine Transportation Safety Authority)** approval. Seawolf was the first brand in Korea to secure this certification. As a specialized domestic life jacket manufacturer, Seawolf consistently leads the market with new safety designs.

​

Compared to the 2023 Seawolf Hero (HS-W06) waist belt model, the new HS-W06BL (Black Label) reduces both weight and bulk to improve wearing comfort.
