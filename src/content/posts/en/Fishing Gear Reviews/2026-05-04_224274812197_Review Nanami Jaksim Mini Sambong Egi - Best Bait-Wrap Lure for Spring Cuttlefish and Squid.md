In this post, I review an excellent egi model for spring cuttlefish and spear squid.

​

Cuttlefish and spear squid lures require several key characteristics. Specifically, using "Sambong egis" (bait-wrap lures) wrapped with dried eel, saury, or squid fillets is highly effective at attracting cephalopods and keeping them holding onto the lure longer.

​

This is why having high-quality bait-wrap lures is essential. Another key trend is the transition towards miniature sizes. Traditional bait-wrap lures were physically large, and while they provided good visibility, they dragged in fast currents and felt unnatural to cautious squid. Modern mini-sized Sambong lures successfully overcome these limitations.

​

A prime example is the **Nanami Jaksim Luminous Mini Sambong Egi**. I purchased two of these lures in each color to prepare for this season's spear squid and cuttlefish trips. The order arrived the day after I placed it.
