​

**💡 Summary & Buying Tips:**

*   For close-range finesse applications with light rigs, select **mini floats (EOS, Pharos)**.
*   For casting distance and high visibility on rough rocky shores, choose the **Wild Fire or Yegam** series.
*   To minimize tangles and cast easily, choosing a **self-standing or semi-self-standing model (GAUSS, Multi-Q)** is highly recommended.
