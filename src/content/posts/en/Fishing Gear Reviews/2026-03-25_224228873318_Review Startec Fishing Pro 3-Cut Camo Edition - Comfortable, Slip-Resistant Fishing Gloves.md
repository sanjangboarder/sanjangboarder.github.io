Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

I participate in most saltwater fishing genres, including charter boat fishing, float fishing, and lure fishing. Regardless of the target species or style, there is one essential item I always pack: fishing gloves.

​

Over more than 10 years of fishing, I have purchased and worn out about two pairs of gloves each year. Based on this experience, I have settled on a few models as my standards. These are the gloves that appear regularly in the photos of my fishing trip reports.

​

Today, I share a review of one of those standard models: the **Startec Fishing Pro 3-Cut Fishing Gloves**. I already own this model in other colors, but recently added the Navy variant to my rotation.

​
