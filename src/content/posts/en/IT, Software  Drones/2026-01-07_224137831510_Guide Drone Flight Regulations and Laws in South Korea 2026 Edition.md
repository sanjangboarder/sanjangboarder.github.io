​

This guide summarizes the legal regulations and procedures required for operating drones (classified as ultra-light flying devices) within South Korea.

#### **1. Qualifications and Device Registration Criteria by Weight**

​

Before flying, you must verify your drone's weight (maximum takeoff weight or MTOW).

​

<div class="single-image">
<img src="https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAxMDJfODMg/MDAxNzY3MzU5MTgxODQ1.9FqwnUNuAQdG-0OjxPX99ANFCE9gw78aTHuHqWOZ8TQg.Rf0UOX_QUFAadSIQdziEAzBAsvhyfLaUdrL03He_fRgg.PNG/image.png?type=w800" alt="Drone weight classification categories" />
</div>

​

**🚁 Under 250g (Micro/Toy Drones)**

* **Representative Models:** DJI Mini series.
* **Certificate:** Not required (*except for commercial operations*).
* **Device Registration:** Exempt.
* **Note:** While regulations are minimal, altitude limits and No-Fly Zones still apply.

​

**🚁 Between 250g and 2kg (Standard Aerial Photography)**

* **Representative Models:** DJI Air, Mavic, and Phantom series.
* **Certificate:** **Category 4 Drone License is mandatory.**
  * You must complete a 6-hour online training course at the Korea Transportation Safety Authority (TS) Education Center.
* **Device Registration:** Exempt for non-commercial (recreational) use.
* **Warning:** Flying without a certificate carries a fine of up to 3,000,000 KRW.

​

**🚁 Over 2kg (Medium to Large Drones)**

* **Certificate:** Category 3 or higher license is mandatory (*requires passing both written and practical examinations*).
* **Device Registration:** **Mandatory.**
  * You must register the device with the Regional Aviation Administration and display the registration number on the drone's body.

#### **2. Flight Location Restrictions (Crucial Step)**

​

Regardless of your drone's weight, **where** you fly is the most critical factor.

​

​

**🚫 No-Fly Zones (Flight Prohibited Areas)**

Flying in these zones is strictly prohibited without prior authorization:

* **P-73 (Seoul central airspace):** Airspace surrounding the Presidential Office and central Seoul.
* **P-518 (Borderline area):** Regions near the Demilitarized Zone (DMZ), including northern Gyeonggi-do and northern Gangwon-do.
* **Nuclear Power Plants:** Space within a 19km radius.

​

**⚠️ Flight Restricted Areas (Controlled Airspace)**

* **Airports and Airfields:** Space within a 9.3km radius.
* **Altitude Limits:** Flying at altitudes exceeding **150m above ground level (AGL)** anywhere in South Korea requires prior approval.

​

**✅ Unrestricted Airspace**

* **Ultra-Light Flying Device Flight Zones (UA):** Space designated for recreational flight without prior approval, subject to a 150m AGL limit and maintaining visual line of sight.
* **General Areas:** Locations not classified as restricted or prohibited zones do not require approval for flights under 150m AGL.

​

> **Recommended Apps:** Use the mobile apps "Ready to Fly" or "DroneFly" to verify whether your current location is open for flight.

#### **3. Aerial Photo Permit Applications**

​

Note that flight approval and photography permits are managed under separate procedures.

​

* **General Rule:** Capturing photos or videos using a drone requires submitting an Aerial Photo Permit Application to the Ministry of National Defense.
* **Relaxed Regulations:** While rules have been partially relaxed for casual recreational photography in open spaces (where no military or national security facilities are present), applying for the permit remains recommended if there is any risk of capturing restricted facilities.
* **How to Apply:** Submit the request via the [Drone One-Stop Civil Service Portal](https://drone.onestop.go.kr) (*applying at least 4 to 7 days before your scheduled flight is recommended*).

#### **4. Pilot Compliance Rules (Fines Apply)**

​

Violating the rules below can result in administrative fines of up to 2,000,000 KRW.

​

<div class="single-image">
<img src="https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAxMDJfMTcz/MDAxNzY3MzU5MjE0MDE5.lXvMIH8nbvMOPbjUsAwsXDRHE5X_Gy742QHHktoMcn0g.dK_o74Krx6epMCTy6wiBfYCHrOb8phty7-E4GYwusGwg.PNG/image.png?type=w800" alt="Pilot safety rules icon set" />
</div>

​

1. **Night Flights Prohibited:** Flying is prohibited between sunset and sunrise (*requires special authorization*).
2. **Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS) Flights Prohibited:** The pilot must maintain direct, unaided visual contact with the drone at all times.
3. **Flying Under the Influence (FUI) Prohibited:** Operating a drone with a blood alcohol content (BAC) of 0.02% or higher is subject to criminal prosecution.
4. **Dropping Objects Prohibited:** Dropping payloads or items from a drone is prohibited.
5. **Dangerous Flights Prohibited:** Flying over crowded areas or at low altitudes near people is prohibited.

#### **Summary Checklist**

​

* [ ] Verify your drone's weight (*complete the Category 4 course if it exceeds 250g*).
* [ ] Verify the airspace using the "Ready to Fly" app.
* [ ] Secure approval via the "Drone One-Stop" portal if flying in No-Fly Zones or at altitudes above 150m AGL.
* [ ] Apply for an "Aerial Photo Permit" if capturing media is your primary goal.
* [ ] Fly only during daylight hours, maintain visual contact, and never fly under the influence.
