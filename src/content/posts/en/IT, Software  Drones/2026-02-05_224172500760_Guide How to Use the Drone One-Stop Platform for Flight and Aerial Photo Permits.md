### **A Complete Guide to Drone One-Stop Services**

​

**'Drone One-Stop'** is an integrated system operated by the Ministry of National Defense and the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport. It allows users to apply for both drone flight and aerial photography permits online.

​

#### **0. Prerequisites (Check before applying)**

1. **Drone Specifications:** Model name, manufacturer, net weight (including battery), and physical dimensions (width x length x height).
2. **Flight Location:** Precise address or coordinates on the map.
3. **Pilot Information:** Name, date of birth, phone number, and pilot certificate number (if applicable).
4. **Application Timing:** You must apply at least **4 business days** before your scheduled flight.

#### **1. Registration and Login**

* **Website:** <https://drone.onestop.go.kr>
* **Sign Up:** While non-member applications are supported, I recommend **registering an account** to easily manage and modify your application history.

**① Flight Approval Application**

* **Required when:** Flying in No-Fly Zones (such as P-73), Controlled Airspace (near airports), or at altitudes above 150m.
* **Note:** Use mobile apps like "Ready to Fly" first to check if your intended location is a restricted zone.

**② Aerial Photo Permit Application (Most Common)**

* **Required when:** Your drone is equipped with a camera and you plan to record video or take photos.
* **Note:** Even if the location does not require a flight permit, you must still apply under this menu to take photos or videos.

#### **3. Step-by-Step Guide to Filling Out the Form**

​

After clicking the application button, complete the four sections in sequence.

​

**[Step 1] Applicant Information**

* This section is pre-filled when logged in. Verify your contact details and check for missing information.

​

**[Step 2] Flight Plan (Key Section)**

​

<div class="single-image">
<img src="https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAxMDJfMjAy/MDAxNzY3MzU5NjUyMjU1.sEw_37d_YLc38C9lmcOOXoeQICzV1Zvr6C59ISWli8sg.Ir7J7zWrVoH4VdkbkagLYs1pmCQfOyIwRLapjN7cNTsg.PNG/image.png?type=w800" alt="Flight plan date and area input" />
</div>

​

* **Flight Period:** You can apply for a maximum duration of six months.
* *Tip: Since weather conditions can prevent flying, avoid requesting a single day. Instead, apply for a window of 1 to 3 months to allow flexibility.*
* **Flight Area Setup:**
  1. Click the **[Register Map]** button on the right.
  2. Search for the address and select the precise coordinates on the map interface.
  3. **Radius (m):** Set a radius of 300m to 500m.
  4. **Altitude (m):** Set to 150m or lower (legal altitude limit).
* **Flight Purpose:** Select "Recreational Flight" or "Hobby."

​

**[Step 3] Drone Device Information**

​

<div class="single-image">
<img src="https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAxMDJfMjAy/MDAxNzY3MzU5Njk0OTMw.lQQDji6YywvMoiSYLsftzwr3wBdXMUarPhyyOyzRm2kg.pGjdtPURA5A-sotoEaQPBYsWCY1qoU9Lr7eVd5xrtR0g.PNG/image.png?type=w800" alt="Drone specifications input form" />
</div>

​

* **Type:** Select "Unmanned Multicopter" (the category for consumer drones).
* **Model/Manufacturer:** Enter your drone's details (e.g., DJI / Mini 3 Pro).
* **Dimensions/Weight:** Refer to your drone's manual and enter the specifications.
* **Owner:** Enter your personal information.

​

**[Step 4] Pilot Information**

* **Pilot:** Check the box "Same as Applicant."
* **Certificate:** For drones exceeding 250g, enter your Category 4 pilot certificate details and attach the corresponding file.

​

#### **4. Application Review**

​

<div class="single-image">
<img src="https://mblogthumb-phinf.pstatic.net/MjAyNjAxMDJfMjcx/MDAxNzY3MzU5NzI2NTE3.Eh1nVV17bPSJ9vWnviwV_lWbgLt7THMoxXpFvHmbMW0g.RJp2tYj8NV3V9lx-WXYoWo-aNXaDwfyjLGJlq8kC_88g.PNG/image.png?type=w800" alt="Submitting the completed form" />
</div>

​

* **Submission:** Click **[Submit]** once all sections are completed.
* **Processing Time:** The application is routed to the competent military unit or aviation authority. The review takes **3 to 4 business days**.
* **Correction Requests:** If there is missing information or security concerns, you will receive a SMS notification requesting corrections. Modify the form on the website and submit it again.

​

#### **5. Verifying Approval**

* **Notification:** You will receive a SMS confirmation once the review is completed.
* **My Page:** Check that the status is marked as "Completed" under [Civil Application Status].
* **Permit:** Click the **[Results]** button to save or print the permit document. You should keep this document with you when flying.
* *Conditional Approvals:* Review the terms carefully, as some permits include conditions like "Photographs require a separate security review prior to release."
