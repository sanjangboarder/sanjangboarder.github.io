Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

‚Äã

The webfoot octopus season is entering its final stage of the year. Since the tide conditions were favorable last Saturday, I booked a morning half-day charter boat out of Incheon Namhang Port, boarding the **Happyho** operated by **Kukje Yuseon**. Incheon boat fishing is well-suited for beginners, and Kukje Yuseon in particular is set up to accommodate those new to the sport.

‚Äã

The office opens around 5:00 AM. For morning charters, the Namhang Recreation Vessel Pier parking lot opens between 5:00 AM and 5:30 AM, allowing for convenient parking if you arrive early. If that area is full, a public parking lot is available nearby.

It had been a while since my last visit to Kukje Yuseon. During the autumn season, half-day morning charters represent a practical option for a quick webfoot octopus trip. After signing the manifest at the office, you can purchase necessary tackle items. Tackle rentals are available for those without their own gear. I brought my full set of gear, but purchased some additional rigs on-site since I was accompanying a beginner.

‚Äã

The half-day hourly charter system on the Happyho is practical for beginners, offering prepared gear, bait, food, and scenic transits.

‚Äã

While the vessel accommodates many beginners, experienced anglers also book these trips. They often secure substantial catches. Booking is generally easier compared to high-speed charter vessels, making it a viable option for experienced anglers looking for a more relaxed trip.

‚Äã

We enjoyed our half-day octopus trip out of Namhang Port. With winter approaching, octopus fishing should remain viable until early December, depending on weather conditions. I hope to manage one additional trip before the season closes.
