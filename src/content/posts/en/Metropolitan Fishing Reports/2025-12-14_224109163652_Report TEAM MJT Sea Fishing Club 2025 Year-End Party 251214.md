Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

This weekend, instead of going fishing, my schedule has been filled with year-end gatherings. Among these was the annual year-end party for the **TEAM MJT sea fishing community**, which I participate in regularly. We prepared a small event to reflect on the 2025 season and spend time with our members.

​

Although TEAM MJT operates as a recreational sea fishing community, our focus on organizing joint outings has naturally led us to prioritize boat fishing in the West Sea. Consequently, a significant portion of our members reside in Incheon, Gimpo, Siheung, Ansan, and Seoul.

​

While our past year-end parties were typically held in Bupyeong, Incheon, we decided to host this year's event near the **Guro Digital Complex** in Seoul.

​

During the 2025 season, TEAM MJT organized approximately 30 regular and flash outings. While we faced multiple cancellations due to weather anomalies and dealt with rain and wind on several trips, we completed the season safely without accidents. While catching fish is satisfying, returning home safely alongside good company remains our primary goal.

​

For 2026, TEAM MJT will continue to organize regular and flash outings focused on boat fishing, cabin float fishing, and coastal foraging in the West Sea. If you are looking to start sea fishing, consider joining us. I look forward to creating new fishing memories with our members in the coming year.

​

Thank you.
