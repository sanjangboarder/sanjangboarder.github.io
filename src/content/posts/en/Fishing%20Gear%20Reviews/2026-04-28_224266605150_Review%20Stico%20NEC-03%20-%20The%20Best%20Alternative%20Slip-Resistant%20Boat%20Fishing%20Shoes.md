Hello, this is SanjangBorder.

​

When going boat fishing, one of the primary concerns is the slippery deck. Sea water, fish mucus, and overflowing water from livewells make boat decks a slip hazard. While I have used various dedicated fishing boots, finding a pair that combines slip resistance and waterproofing with breathability is difficult.

​

I realized that a change of perspective was needed. Because dedicated fishing boots extend above the ankle, their structure limits ventilation. I needed shoes that were easy to put on, breathable, kept splashed water out, and prevented slips on wet surfaces.

​

I was unable to find a suitable option among dedicated fishing shoes. However, kitchen and culinary work shoes are designed for waterproofing and slip resistance on wet restaurant floors. The **Stico NEC-03** caught my attention.

​

Although marketed as a kitchen shoe, I expected its features would translate well to saltwater boat decks. Today, I share my review after testing it during boat trips.

​
